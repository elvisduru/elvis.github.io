Target Audience

We have in our target, audience who are realtors, who invest in properties by selling and buying. We also target property owners who have properties that they may want to sell or rent, and we also target tenants who are currently seeking accomodation or any one in particular that wants to own a property. We have a referral system as well and those who refer our services can benefit greatly from our business.

We are technologically driven meaning a majority segment of our market are going to be those who are very familiar with the internet.
